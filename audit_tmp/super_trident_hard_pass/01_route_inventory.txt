Legacy / broad still present in filesystem (middleware redirects PAGE routes to /trident/cases or /trident/settings for some prefixes, but routes still SHIP in bundle):
- /exec, /edi, /fax, /revenue, /intake, /matia, /executive, /ceo, /founder, /lite/patients, /patients/[patientId], /settings/wiki, /admin/... (many)
- **API route handlers** under frontend/src/app/api/ are **NOT** in middleware redirect list — 90+ route.ts files (edi, denials, fax, exec, availity, patients, orders, etc.) remain in codebase.

## Middleware (frontend/middleware.ts)
- redirectToCases: /, /ceo, /edi, /exec, /executive, /fax, /founder, /intake, /matia, /patients, /revenue  → /trident/cases
- redirectToSettings: /admin, /settings  → /trident/settings
- /lite  → /trident  rewrite
- matcher **excludes** login, api/auth, api/health, api/public-inquiry, api/core, _next, favicon — **all other /api/* except the excluded ones still go through withAuth and can be invoked if a session token exists** (e.g. /api/edi, /api/patients, /api/denials).

## Trident nav (trident/layout.tsx)

No EDI/claims in this nav, but they exist elsewhere in repo.
