CarePath Operating Workflow

01

Clinical Trigger

Surgery, injury, birth, discharge, or mobility event initiates the pathway.

02

Eligibility Verification

Payer, plan, benefit type, coverage, and prior authorization requirements confirmed.

03

Documentation Control

SWO, clinical notes, medical necessity, and packet generation governed by Trident.

04

Product-Pathway Selection

Medically necessary recovery products matched to verified benefit and documentation.

05

Fulfillment + POD Capture

Coordinated delivery with signed proof-of-delivery and tracking confirmation.

06

Billing-Ready Packet

Complete packet submitted. Patient held in system. Next care node activated.
