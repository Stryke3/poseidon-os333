Adam Stryker is a healthcare operator and founder of StrykeFox Medical, focused on medical devices, DME, biologics, physician enablement, reimbursement infrastructure, and healthcare platform development.

He builds integrated healthcare infrastructure across surgical supply, technology-driven operations, physician relationships, and scalable operating systems designed for long-term value creation across regulated healthcare markets.

“Healthcare infrastructure is not a product category — it is a systems architecture problem. The operators who win will be the ones who build compounding platforms, not point solutions.”
